Nobel Laureates Data Analysis¶

Step I: Understnding the problem statement¶

The goal of this is to analyse the Nobel Laureates dataset to gain insight into their lives, demographics and categories they were awarded for

Step II: Understanding the dataset¶

The dataset contains details about their names, gender, where and when they were born, where and they died, whch unversity they studied in, the category they got the Nobel Prize for and when and lastly their motivation to do so.

Step III: Importing the dataset¶

We load the datset into a pandas dataframe and to help us easily make insights, understand trends and visualise them using the Matplotlib library. For this we read the CSV and upload the data to a Pandas datset.

Step IV: Data Exploration, Selection, Cleaning¶

We try to identify duplicate rows, with Null values and remove them.

Step V: Data Analysis and Visualisation¶

We try to brainstorm and answer questions about this dataset and see trends into it and see which might help with visualisation will make it clear.

Questions Answered¶

Q1. The count of laureates in each category ?
Q2. The distribution of laureates by gender ?
Q3. How many laureates might have had foreign education ?
Q4. How many laureates died abroad ?
Q5. Which university produced the most Nobel laureates ?
Q6. In which country were the most Nobel laureates born ?
Q7. How many Nobel Laureates were awarded the Nobel Prize posthumously ?
Q8. The average lifespan of Nobel Laureates ?
Q9. What are the most common first names of Nobel Laureates ?
